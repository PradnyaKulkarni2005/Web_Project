BIHAR


Embark on a Journey Through Cultural Splendor and Historical Marvels.

Discover Bihar, a hidden gem in the heart of India, where history, spirituality, and culture converge. Home to ancient Nalanda University, the sacred Bodh Gaya where Buddha attained enlightenment, and the stunning architecture of the Mahabodhi Temple, Bihar offers an unforgettable journey through time. From vibrant festivals to tranquil rivers, lush green landscapes to bustling markets, Bihar invites you to explore its rich heritage and warm hospitality. Embark on an adventure that promises to be as diverse as it is enriching. Welcome to Bihar, where every corner tells a story!

Historical and Cultural Sites

Mahabodhi Temple, Bodh Gaya

A UNESCO World Heritage site where Buddha attained enlightenment.

Nalanda University

Ancient seat of learning and one of the world's oldest universities.

Pawapuri

Jain pilgrimage site where Lord Mahavira attained Nirvana.

Vaishali

Archaeological site associated with Lord Buddha and the ancient Licchavi Republic.

Religious Sites

Mahavir Mandir, Patna

Famous Hanuman temple in Patna.

Patna Sahib Gurudwara

Birthplace of Guru Gobind Singh, the tenth Sikh Guru.

Kesaria Stupa

One of the tallest Buddhist stupas in the world.

Sita Kund, Sitamarhi

Mythological site related to Sita from Ramayana.

Natural and Scenic Beauty

Valmiki National Park

Bihar's only tiger reserve, known for its lush forests and wildlife.

Kakolat Falls, Nawada

Scenic waterfall surrounded by greenery.

Rajgir

Known for its hot springs and scenic beauty.

Architectural Marvels

Patna Museum

Houses a rich collection of Mauryan and Gupta stone sculptures.

Sher Shah Suri Masjid

Remarkable architectural blend of Afghan and Indian styles.

Rohtas Fort

A UNESCO World Heritage site, known for its fortifications and historical significance.

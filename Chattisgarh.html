CHATTISGARH


"A Journey Through Cultural Riches and Natural Wonders"

Welcome to Chhattisgarh, India's hidden gem brimming with natural beauty, rich culture, and exhilarating adventures! Nestled in the heart of the country, Chhattisgarh offers pristine waterfalls, lush green forests, and ancient temples that beckon travelers seeking serenity and thrill alike. Discover the vibrant tribal heritage, explore untamed wildlife in expansive national parks, and immerse yourself in the warm hospitality of this captivating land. Whether you're an adventurer, a history enthusiast, or a nature lover, Chhattisgarh promises a unique and unforgettable experience. Come, uncover the secrets of this enchanting state!

Historical and Cultural Sites

Kanker Palace

A beautiful heritage palace showcasing royal architecture.

Chitrakote Falls

Known as the 'Niagara of India,' these falls are culturally significant and breathtakingly beautiful.

Sirpur

An archaeological site with ancient temples and monasteries, showcasing the state's rich historical heritage.

Wildlife and Nature Reserves

Barnawapara Wildlife Sanctuary

Known for its diverse wildlife and scenic beauty.

Indravati National Park

Famous for its population of the endangered wild buffalo.

Udanti Wildlife Sanctuary

Home to the rare wild buffalo and diverse flora and fauna.

Adventure and trekking

Mainpat

Often referred to as the "Shimla of Chhattisgarh," it's a perfect spot for trekking and nature walks.

Kotumsar Cave

Ideal for spelunking and exploring subterranean limestone formations.

Dandami Luxury Resort

Located near the Chitrakote Falls, offering adventure activities like boating and trekking.

Religious Sites

Bhoramdeo Temple

Often called the 'Khajuraho of Chhattisgarh,' this temple is known for its exquisite carvings.

Dongargarh

Famous for the Bambleshwari Temple located on a hilltop.

Rajim

Known for its group of ancient temples and its annual Kumbh Mela.

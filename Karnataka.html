KARNATAKA


The Jewel of South India

Welcome to Karnataka, a mesmerizing blend of ancient heritage and natural beauty. From the majestic palaces of Mysore and the historic ruins of Hampi to the lush Western Ghats and serene beaches of Gokarna, Karnataka offers a diverse tapestry of experiences. Adventure seekers, history buffs, and nature lovers will find endless opportunities to explore and immerse themselves in this captivating state. Discover the vibrant culture, savor the delectable cuisine, and let the enchanting landscapes of Karnataka leave you spellbound.

Historical and Cultural Sites

Hampi

A UNESCO World Heritage site, known for its ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire.

Mysore Palace

A stunning example of Indo-Saracenic architecture, located in Mysore.

Badami

Famous for its rock-cut temples and cave temples.

Belur and Halebidu

Renowned for their exquisite Hoysala architecture and intricate temple carvings.

Natural Attractions

Coorg (Kodagu)

Known for its coffee plantations, lush green landscapes, and scenic beauty.

Nandi Hills

A popular hill station offering panoramic views and a pleasant climate.

Jog Falls

One of the highest waterfalls in India, located in the Shimoga district.

Bandipur National Park

A famous wildlife sanctuary known for its tiger reserve.

Religious Sites

Shravanabelagola

A major Jain pilgrimage site, famous for the giant monolithic statue of Gommateshwara.

Udupi

Known for the famous Krishna Temple and its delicious cuisine.

Gokarna

A pilgrimage town with beautiful beaches and the famous Mahabaleshwar Temple.

Murudeshwar

Home to the towering statue of Lord Shiva and a popular beach destination.

Adventure and Trekking

Chikmagalur

Ideal for trekking, coffee plantations, and serene hill stations.

Kudremukh

Known for its trekking trails and biodiversity.

Agumbe Lake

Popular for its rainforest and trekking trails, often referred to as the "Cherrapunji of the South."

Urban and Modern Attractions

Bangalore

The capital city, known for its parks, nightlife, shopping, and modern architecture.

Mysore

Besides the Mysore Palace, it is known for the Brindavan Gardens and Chamundi Hill.

Wildlife Sanctuaries and Waterfalls

Kabini Wildlife Sanctuary

Known for its rich wildlife and elephant sightings.

Nagarhole National Park

Famous for its tiger and elephant reserves.

Shivanasamudra Falls

A segmented waterfall on the Kaveri River, known for its beauty.

Abbey Falls

Located in Coorg, surrounded by coffee plantations and spice estates.

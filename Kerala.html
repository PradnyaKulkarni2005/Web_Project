KERALA


"Where Nature Meets Culture - Explore India's Serene Backwaters and Lush Landscapes"

Welcome to Kerala, the enchanting "God's Own Country," where lush green landscapes, serene backwaters, and golden beaches await your discovery. Nestled in the southwestern coast of India, Kerala offers a captivating blend of natural beauty, rich culture, and warm hospitality. From the tranquil tea plantations of Munnar to the vibrant festivals and rejuvenating Ayurvedic therapies, every corner of this paradise promises a unique and unforgettable experience. Embark on a journey to Kerala, where nature's splendor and cultural richness come together to create a perfect getaway.

Hill Stations

Munnar

Known for its tea gardens, misty hills, and Eravikulam National Park.

Wayanad

Famous for its wildlife sanctuaries, waterfalls, and caves.

Thekkady

Renowned for Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary and spice plantations.

Vagamon

Offers lush greenery, pine forests, and trekking trails.

Backwaters

Alleppey (Alappuzha)

Known as the "Venice of the East," it offers houseboat cruises and scenic backwaters.

Kumarakom

Famous for its bird sanctuary and backwater resorts.

Kollam

Offers picturesque backwaters and the Ashtamudi Lake.

Beaches

Kovalam

Popular for its crescent-shaped beaches and lighthouse.

Varkala

Known for its cliffs and Papanasam beach.

Marari Beach

A serene and less crowded beach ideal for relaxation.

Bekal

Famous for its fort and beautiful beach.

Wildlife Sanctuaries and National Parks

Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary

Known for its elephants and boat safaris.

Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary

Offers diverse flora and fauna.

Silent Valley National Park

A rich rainforest with unique biodiversity.

Chinnar Wildlife Sanctuary

Known for its endangered species and trekking options.

Cultural and Historical Sites

Fort Kochi

Offers a mix of colonial architecture, Chinese fishing nets, and historic churches.

Thrissur

Famous for the Vadakkunnathan Temple and Thrissur Pooram festival.

Padmanabhapuram Palace

Known for its wooden architecture and intricate carvings.

Pilgrimage Sites

Sabarimala

One of the most famous pilgrimage centers in South India.

Guruvayur

Renowned for the Guruvayur Temple dedicated to Lord Krishna.

Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple

A significant Hindu temple in Thiruvananthapuram.

Waterfalls

Athirappilly Waterfalls

Known as the "Niagara of India."

Meenmutty Falls

Located in Wayanad, known for its breathtaking beauty.

Soochipara Falls

Also in Wayanad, offering picturesque views and trekking opportunities.

Adventure and Nature Activities

Paragliding in Vagamon

Ideal for thrill-seekers.

Trekking in Agasthyakoodam

One of the highest peaks in Kerala.

Snake Boat Races in Alleppey

Experience the thrilling boat races, especially during the Nehru Trophy Boat Race.

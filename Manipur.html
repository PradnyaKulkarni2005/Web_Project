MANIPUR


A Gateway to Unexplored Northeast India

Manipur is a hidden gem waiting to be explored. Known as the "Jewel of India," this enchanting state boasts breathtaking landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and warm hospitality. From the serene Loktak Lake, where floating islands of phumdis mesmerize visitors, to the vibrant markets of Imphal bustling with traditional handicrafts and delicacies, Manipur offers a unique blend of natural beauty and cultural diversity. Whether you're trekking through lush forests, immersing yourself in Manipuri dance and music, or savoring the flavors of its cuisine, every moment in Manipur promises an unforgettable adventure. Discover the unexplored charm of Manipur and create memories that will last a lifetime.

Natural Attractions

Loktak Lake

The largest freshwater lake in northeastern India, famous for its floating phumdis (heterogeneous mass of vegetation, soil, and organic matter).

Keibul Lamjao National Park

The world's only floating national park, home to the endangered Sangai deer.

Cultural and Historical Sites

Shree Govindajee Temple

An important Vaishnavite temple located next to the Manipur Royal Palace in Imphal.

Kangla Fort

The historic fort and palace complex that was the traditional seat of the rulers of Manipur.

Adventure and Outdoor Activities

Dzukou Valley

Known for its pristine natural beauty, the valley is a popular trekking destination.

Tharon Cave

A fascinating cave with intricate limestone formations, perfect for spelunking and adventure.

Scenic Towns and Villages

Ukhrul

A picturesque hill station known for its beautiful landscapes and vibrant culture of the Tangkhul Nagas.

Moirang

A historic town known for the INA Memorial Complex and its association with the Indian freedom struggle.

Handicrafts and Local Markets

Khwairamband Bazaar

Asia's largest all-women's market, offering a wide range of local handicrafts and traditional items.

Paona Bazaar

A bustling market in Imphal known for its variety of Manipuri textiles and handloom products.
